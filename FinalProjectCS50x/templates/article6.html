Wizards guard Delon Wright out until December with sprained left knee

The team says Wright's injury will be treated conservatively and the 9-year NBA veteran will be re-evaluated in 3 weeks

The Washington Wizards announced Saturday that backup guard Delon Wright has a sprained left knee and will be re-evaluated in three weeks. The team said the injury would be treated “conservatively” after Wright was hurt in the fourth quarter of Friday night’s loss to Charlotte. The 31-year-old Wright has averaged 17.6 minutes and 5.0 points per game this season. Washington plays at Brooklyn on Sunday.
